# Murphmon

[MurphLeague 2026](https://github.com/TakeJoshyy/TheMurphVerse/tree/main/2.Tournaments/MurphLeague-2026/Murphleague-2026) Tourney #4 is Emerald Murphmon - Summer Edition. Base rules and information found [here](https://github.com/TakeJoshyy/TheMurphVerse/tree/main/1.GameModes/Murphmon). 

Murphmon is a randomized Pokemon challenge inspired by Super Kaizo IronMON. It aims to be a bit easier and faster while adding more decision making opportunities to the game. Murphmon can be played 10 different ways, all of which remain officially unbeaten in their base rulesets. Many rules and settings are mindfully adapted for tournament play and the game is intended to be challenging but beatable.

"Summer Edition" will feature a pool of 7 Summer Themed 'Tune-ups'. Competitors will be given 3 'Tuning Tokens' per seed that they may spend on Tune-Ups. 

This Season's Tune-Ups are:
|         Tune-Ups          | Details |
|-|-|
| Harvester | You may grab 1 tree of berries from each soil patch en route to safari. Collection cutoff is the Safari building. Berries may be used on pre- and/or post-pivot mons. |           
| Hydration  | You may use drink heals outside of battle until Safari. (Fresh water, Soda Pop, Lemonade, Berry Juice)|
| Vacation | Re-randomize the seed! Hard save in game, set your attempts back 1, and re-roll the seed prior to fighting the first aqua grunt. Vacation counts as 3 tune-ups in the early game pivot. If used at Safari, only counts as 1 tune-up. However, you must have 6 pivots locked in BEFORE using Vacation. No further catches are allowed after Vacationing (i.e. searching for shinies/favorites) |
| Band Camp   | Unlock all flutes and bells for mons with a quad weakness.  |
| Gone Surfin'   | You may teach and use Surf in battle with a Safari pivot that is under 500 BST and not water type |
| Farmer's Market  |  You may purchase a bundle of vitamins from the Slateport market, must be all one transaction and before going north. |
| No Hoenn Around | You may forego early pivot options to gain 3 more wild exp kills. No favorites/shiny exceptions. You must commit to this tune-up and obtain your additional EXP prior to the first youngster fight. |

# Tourney Details

T4 is hosted by [roflmaogamers1](https://www.twitch.tv/roflmaogamers1). Dates: July 2nd 11:59 PM EST - July 19th 11:59 PM EST. Sign up deadline is July 10th with details on tourney sign up available in the MurphVerse Discord.

> You must be in the [MurphVerse discord](https://discord.gg/fNX4geCU) and all seeds must be live for the entire run and have a VOD available. Murphleague tourneys are free to enter and open to all entrants unless previously banned.

Competitors will be given 100 seeds. Your score is determined by your 3 highest scoring runs combined.

## Awards

Upon entering this tournament you will claim which charity you are playing for. BigMurph619 will be providing donation to each of the top 3 finishers. Additionally, any donations from the "Pledge Page" in Murph's discord will be added to the prize pool.

Competitors will also earn "League Points" towards the MurphLeague according to their respective placement. League Points determine eligibility and seeding for the Murphleague playoffs which has a larger prize pool including charity donations, an engraved trophy, and more.
