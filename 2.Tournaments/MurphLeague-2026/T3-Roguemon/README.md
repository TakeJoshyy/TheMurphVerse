# Roguemon

[MurphLeague 2026](https://github.com/TakeJoshyy/TheMurphVerse/tree/main/2.Tournaments/MurphLeague-2026/Murphleague-2026) Tourney #3 is Roguemon Ascension 2. Base rules and information found [here](https://github.com/Crozwords/Roguemon).

RogueMon makes FRLG into a full Randomized Pokemon Roguelike. The goal is to win by locking every pokemon type and finishing a run without fainting. On your journey, you'll receive prizes to help you on your way while curses will hinder your path.

# Tourney Details

T3 is hosted by [Bemidgegaming](https://www.twitch.tv/bemidgegaming). Dates: April 30th 11:59 PM EST - May 17th 11:59 PM EST. Sign up deadline is May 8th.

> You must be in the [MurphVerse discord](https://discord.gg/fNX4geCU) and all seeds must be live for the entire run and have a VOD available. Murphleague tourneys are free to enter and open to all entrants unless previously banned.

Competitors will select 6 types to run up to 20 attempts each in. Your highest scoring run from each type will be added together to get your cumulative score. You must NOT choose any of the 6 types you selected for T1 (if you participated). You may NOT run any of your selected types again for the remainder of the Murphleague 2026 season. Typeless is banned from your 6 choices.

## Awards

Upon entering this tournament you will claim which charity you are playing for. BigMurph619 will be providing donation to each of the top 3 finishers. Additionally, any donations from the "Pledge Page" in Murph's discord will be added to the prize pool.

Competitors will also earn "League Points" towards the MurphLeague according to their respective placement. League Points determine eligibility and seeding for the Murphleague playoffs which has a larger prize pool including charity donations, an engraved trophy, and more.
